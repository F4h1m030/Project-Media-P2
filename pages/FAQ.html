FAQ

Game Downloads FAQ

1. How do I download a game?
Click on the "Download" button on the game's page. The download will start automatically.
2. Are the games free to download?
Most games are free to download, but some may require a purchase or subscription.
3. What platforms are supported?
Our games are available for Windows, macOS, and some are compatible with Linux. Mobile versions are also available for iOS and Android.
4. How can I report a bug in a game?
You can report bugs by visiting our support page and filling out the bug report form.
